Common Workflows

  • Lead-to-cash: marketing attribution, opportunity sync, billing trigger, renewal visibility.
  • Ticket-to-renewal: support health feeds account strategy and customer success motions.
  • Quote-to-invoice: approved quote data flows directly into finance systems.
  • Identity/auth provisioning: role, access, and account state changes stay consistent.
  • Product usage to CRM scoring: usage events map to lifecycle and expansion actions.
