Case Study

Field services quote-to-cash integration

HubSpot development and middleware orchestration to connect quoting, invoicing, and downstream finance operations.

  • CRM: HubSpot
  • Integration: Middleware
  • Industry: Services
  • Timeline: 10 weeks

Problem

Operations teams manually moved quote data into billing and ERP tools. Sync failures created invoice delays, handoff errors, and limited visibility into renewal status.

Approach

Standardized pipeline stages, introduced middleware orchestration with queue-based retries, and deployed alerting for failed records plus SLA-driven remediation.

Stack

HubSpot, Workato, NetSuite, Stripe, Slack, and Snowflake reporting sync.

Timeline

10 weeks total: discovery and mapping (2), architecture (2), build and QA (5), launch (1).

Results

  • 52% reduction in manual ops handoffs.
  • 29% faster invoicing cycle time.
  • 31% lower billing exception rate after go-live.

Client Quote

"The integration work eliminated duplicate entry and gave leadership live pipeline visibility."
