Case Study

AI-assisted sales reporting dashboard

An anonymized reporting example that turned messy sales activity and call notes into useful management insight.

  • Workflow: AI-assisted reporting
  • Integration: Activity data + dashboard
  • Industry: Operations-heavy services
  • Pattern: Discovery, report build, leadership review

Problem

Leadership needed a clearer view of salesperson activity, outreach volume, sales outcomes, revenue trends, and underperformance patterns hidden inside unstructured notes and activity data.

Approach

Built an AI-assisted sales reporting dashboard that summarized activity, grouped patterns, and translated raw operational data into practical management insight.

Stack

CRM exports, call/activity notes, AI-assisted summarization, structured reporting views, and owner-ready dashboard presentation.

Timeline

Iterative delivery through reporting discovery, data review, dashboard build, and presentation to ownership, finance, and operations stakeholders.

What Changed

  • Unstructured sales activity became a readable management dashboard.
  • Owners and managers could compare outreach, revenue trends, and coaching opportunities.
  • The report delivered deeper operational insight without expanding the original timeline.

Why It Matters

AI is most useful when it turns messy operational data into decisions managers can act on, not when it adds another tool to check.
