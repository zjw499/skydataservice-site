Case Study

Fintech CRM + billing sync

Custom CRM architecture and ETL/ELT integration program to restore forecast trust and unify revenue reporting.

  • CRM: Custom
  • Integration: ETL/ELT + API
  • Industry: Financial Services
  • Timeline: 12 weeks

Problem

Billing events and CRM opportunity stages diverged across systems, causing missed expansion signals and low confidence in weekly forecast reporting.

Approach

Built a canonical revenue event model, implemented near-real-time CRM updates, and added warehouse reconciliation jobs with exception dashboards.

Stack

Custom CRM modules, API gateway, event bus, Snowflake, billing platform, and executive reporting layer.

Timeline

12 weeks total: discovery (2), architecture and model design (2), build and testing (6), launch and optimization (2).

Results

  • Forecast accuracy improved from 71% to 93%.
  • Manual reconciliation time reduced by 58%.
  • Revenue exception detection improved with same-day alerts.

Client Quote

"The new integration architecture gave our leadership team one version of the truth for pipeline and billing."

Director of Revenue Operations, Fintech
