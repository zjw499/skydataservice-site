About

A practical CRM operations partner for teams that need adopted systems

We help growing teams replace messy CRM, Microsoft, AI, and reporting workarounds with clear workflows users can actually run.

How we deliver

  • Start with discovery around real user behavior
  • Build with security, permissions, and maintainability
  • Use AI where it improves triage or visibility
  • Document what your team needs to run it

Mission

Help growing businesses run sales, service, and operations on CRM systems they can trust and maintain.

Values

Clear plans, accountable delivery, secure access, practical documentation, and quality in every release.

Why Sky Data Services

Founder-led delivery that connects CRM, Microsoft Graph, OpenAI automation, integrations, dashboards, and adoption support.

Delivery Approach

A lightweight, founder-led team that works directly with business owners, operations leaders, managers, and frontline users.

Workflow Discovery

Focus: Translate non-technical pain into Salesforce tools, integration maps, dashboards, and rollout priorities.

Method: Interview users, inspect current workarounds, and design around the job they already do.

Operations-Aware Builds

Focus: Bring practical frontline healthcare and public-safety operations perspective to workflow design, documentation, and adoption.

Method: Reduce unnecessary disruption and show users how new systems improve the work they already do.

AI-Assisted Iteration

Focus: Use OpenAI, Notion, Microsoft Graph, and reporting workflows to speed triage and make feedback actionable.

Method: Pair sandbox testing with annotated guides, screenshots, release notes, and controlled deployment loops.
